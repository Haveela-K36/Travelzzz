3 Days in Paris

A whirlwind tour of the City of Lights.

  • Day 1: Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, Seine River Cruise
  • Day 2: Notre Dame Cathedral, Latin Quarter, Montmartre
  • Day 3: Palace of Versailles (optional), Shopping, Farewell Dinner

7 Days in Tokyo

Explore the vibrant capital of Japan.

  • Day 1: Shibuya, Harajuku, Meiji Shrine
  • Day 2: Asakusa, Senso-ji Temple, Tokyo Skytree
  • Day 3: Shinjuku, Tokyo Metropolitan Government Building, Robot Restaurant
  • Day 4: Day trip to Hakone (hot springs, views of Mt. Fuji)
  • Day 5: Ginza (shopping), Imperial Palace East Garden
  • Day 6: Ueno Park (museums, zoo), Akihabara (electronics)
  • Day 7: Explore local neighborhoods, farewell dinner

5 Days in Rome

Discover the ancient wonders of Rome.

  • Day 1: Colosseum, Roman Forum, Palatine Hill
  • Day 2: Vatican City (St. Peter's Basilica, Vatican Museums)
  • Day 3: Trevi Fountain, Pantheon, Spanish Steps
  • Day 4: Borghese Gallery and Gardens, Catacombs
  • Day 5: Trastevere neighborhood, Cooking class (optional)
